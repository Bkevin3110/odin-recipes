Long Island

Description

This is a classic, and one of my all time favorite recipes. It's quick, easy, and incredibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 oz. Vodka
  • 1⁄2 oz. London Dry gin
  • 1⁄2 oz. White rum
  • 1⁄2 oz. Silver tequila
  • 1⁄2 oz. Cointreau
  • 3⁄4 oz. Fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tsp. 2:1 simple syrup
  • 3⁄4 oz. Coca-cola

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except Coca-cola into a shaker
  2. Add ice to the shaker and shake to combine
  3. Get a collins glass and add ice to it
  4. Strain ingredients into collins glass
  5. Top collins glass with Coca-Cola and garnish with a lemon twist
